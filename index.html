Our Facilities

Everything a cricketer needs to grow their game — all under one roof.

Practice Nets

Professional-grade batting and bowling nets for focused skill development with coaches supervising every session.

Indoor Tape Ball

Sheltered indoor facility available for tape ball cricket training and private bookings year-round.

Day-Night Lights

High-powered floodlights enabling sessions into the evening, simulating real match conditions after dark.

Professional Coaching

Certified, experienced coaches who tailor training to individual players — from grip to game strategy.
